Our Story

Who We Are

Hannan Stationers Bhaddar was built in 2012 with a simple goal: to become a trusted, friendly, and well-stocked store for the local community. What started as a small stationery shop has now grown into a complete general store serving the daily needs of families in the area.

We take pride in serving our customers with care, offering quality products, fair prices, and a smooth shopping experience. For us, every customer is part of our growing community, and we aim to make shopping easy and reliable for everyone.

Our Mission

To deliver a complete shopping experience by offering stationery items, groceries, sports goods, and daily-use products in one place. We aim to ensure quality products, fair prices, and friendly customer service so that every customer leaves satisfied.
